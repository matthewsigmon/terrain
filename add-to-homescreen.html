Add Terrain to Your Home Screen

For the best experience, add Terrain as an app. It will run full screen without any browser bars.

iPhone & iPad (Safari)

  1. Open terrainapp.org in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow pointing up)
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Tap Add in the top right
  5. Launch Terrain from your home screen

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open terrainapp.org in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top right)
  3. Tap Add to Home screen or Install app
  4. Confirm by tapping Add
  5. Launch Terrain from your home screen
Why do this? When launched from your home screen, Terrain runs as a standalone app with no address bar or browser controls—just your content, full screen.
